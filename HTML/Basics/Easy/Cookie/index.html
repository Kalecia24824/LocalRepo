Kalecia's Strawberry Cookies

Welcome! This recipe will show you how to make strawberry cookies

Ingredients

Here are the ingredients for the cookies:

  • Strawberries (Freeze Dried)
  • Flour
  • 1 egg
  • Butter
  • Sugar
  • Red Food Coloring (Optional)

Instructions

Follow these instructions to make the cookies:

  1. Crush the freeze-dried strawberries into a powder using a food processor or any other method. But leave out 1 tablespoon for rolling later.

  2. Mix the butter and sugar together with a mixer of your choice for 2 minutes into a bowl.

  3. Stir the strawberry powder except for the reserved tablespoon to make the batter red. (You can also add food coloring if you are using it.)

  4. Crack the egg to add it to the batter and mix it until combined.

  5. In a separate bowl, add the flour and the strawberry powder. Mix them together until the bowl is red. Then add the dry bowl into the mixture in three equal parts.

  6. Once all the ingredients are added together then add 1/4 cup of sugar into the mixture. Then, grab 1 and 1/2 tablespoon size of dough and roll them through the sugar bowl.

  7. Place the dough balls 2 inches apart on a parchment lined sheet and bake for 11 minutes at 350ÂºF.

  8. Once the cookies are done, let them cool on the sheet before putting them in a cooling place for a few minutes. Then, you are done!


Some things to note:

  • Remember that each batch will differ depending on how many cookies you are planning to make.

  • Also, do not overbake the cookies because if you do then they will become dry and lose their strawberry taste.

  • Referring from the last point, you must let your baking sheet cool in between batches. Otherwise, you can have runny cookies which is not good.
