Lasagna Recipe

This lasagna recipe is delicious with ricotta cheese, savory meat and mozzarella cheese. Enjoy!

Prepping: 40 mins

Cooking: 45 mins

Cooling: 15 mins

Total: 1 hr and 40 mins

Servings: 12 people


Ingredients:

All directions will be in US measurement!

Cheese Filling:

  • 15 oz of ricotta cheese, 2 cups
  • 1 egg (large)
  • 2 cups of mozzarella cheese
  • 3/4 cup of Parmesan cheese (freshly grated)
  • 2 tsp of Italian seasoning
  • 1/2 tsp of salt
  • 1/4 tsp of pepper

Meat Sauce:

  • 1 tbsp of olive oil
  • 1 yellow onion (finely diced)
  • 3/4 lb. of ground beef
  • 3/4 lb. of ground Italian sausage
  • 3 cloves of garlic
  • 1/2 cup of chicken broth
  • 40 oz (5 cups) of marinara sauce
  • 1 tbsp of tomato paste
  • 1 tsp of hot sauce
  • 1 tsp of Worcestershire sauce

Lasagna:

  • 12 lasagna noodles (Add extra in case!)
  • 2 1/2 cup of mozzarella cheese

Required Utensils

  • 1 medium bowl
  • A 9 x 13 casserole dish or pan substitute for the lasagna
  • Spatula
  • Wooden Spoon
  • 3 pots (2 medium and 1 large)
  • 1 knife
  • Wax or Parchment Paper

Instructions

Prepping

  1. Combine the cheese filling ingredients in a medium-sized bowl and set it aside for later.

  2. Measure out the rest of the ingredients.

Making the Meat Sauce

  1. Heat the olive oil over medium heat in the first pot and add the diced onions. Let the onion soften for 10 mins.

  2. Add the ground beef and sausage then increase the heat to a medium-high. Use a spatula to break up the meat until it is very fine.

  3. Cook for 8 to 10 mins (or until it is cooked through) before adding the garlic during the last minute. Drain the excess grease after the mixture is done.

  4. In the second medium pot, add the chicken broth. Then add the marinara sauce, tomato paste, hot sauce, and the Worcestershire sauce. Bring the pot to a boil then reduce it to a simmer gently while boiling the noodles

Pre-heating Oven and Cooking the Noddles

  1. Preheat oven to 375º degrees

  2. In the last and large pot, add some salt with water to boil it for the noddles.

  3. Once the pot rapidly boils, cook the noodles to al denté according to your package. Then gently stir with a wooden spoon while cooking so the noodles won't stick.

  4. Once the noddles are cooked, drain and rinse them with cold water until the noodles have cooled.

  5. Lay the cool noodles flat on wax or parchment paper once you assemble the lasagna.

Assembling

  1. Spread 1 cup of meat sauce on the bottom of the casserole/pan. Then add 4 lasagna noodles.

  2. Add 1/3 of the ricotta cheese mixture over the noodles. Then add 1 1/2 cups of meat sauce.

  3. Repeat the process again until you reach the top where you will top the lasagna with 2 1.2 cups of Mozzarella cheese.

  4. Then spray foil with a non-stick cooking spray and place it spray side down on top of the baking dish.

Baking

  1. Bake the dish for 35 minutes. Then remove the foil cover before baking for 10 minutes

  2. Once the dish is done, then remove foil and garnish with parsley. Let it sit for 15 minutes before serving.
